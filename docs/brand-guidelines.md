# Allay House Brand Guidelines

> Version 1.1 · Neutral palette correction · 6 July 2026

## Brand idea

Allay House is a refined sanctuary where beauty, wellness, and movement live under one calm roof. It is the parent house for Allay Spa, Allay Pilates, Allay Nail Studio, Allay Lash Studio, Allay Salon, and future self-care divisions.

**Positioning:** considered care for the whole self.

**Primary message:** A softer place to return to yourself.

**Supporting message:** Beauty, wellness, and movement—thoughtfully held under one house.

## Voice

| We are | We are not |
| --- | --- |
| Warm and assured | Over-familiar or excitable |
| Calm and clear | Vague or overly poetic |
| Sensory and considered | Flowery or indulgent |
| Expert but gentle | Clinical or patronising |

Use short, spacious sentences. Prefer “your appointment is reserved” to “you’re all booked!” and “choose a time that suits you” to “grab your slot.”

The interface is predominantly ivory, cream, stone, beige, taupe, and espresso. Green and mauve never define large surfaces or primary brand moments. Avoid bright colours, pure white as a dominant canvas, harsh black, and cool corporate greys.

## Typography

- Display: Cormorant Garamond, 500 weight. Editorial, soft, and high contrast.
- Body/UI: Inter with Manrope fallback, 400–600 weight. Clean and highly legible.
- Labels: Inter 600, uppercase, generous tracking.

Display headings use restrained line lengths and sentence case. Body copy should rarely exceed 65 characters per line.

## Imagery

Use natural, diffused light; tactile linen, limewash, stone, pale timber, skin, and subtle botanical forms. Frames can use generous rounded corners or an arch silhouette. Keep compositions quiet and editorial, with negative space.

Avoid product-grid photography, clinical treatment imagery, staged influencer poses, visible third-party branding, heavy candle motifs, or glossy CGI perfection.

## UI principles

- Spacious before decorative.
- One clear primary action per section.
- Rounded rectangles for utility; arches for editorial imagery.
- Motion should feel like breath: subtle opacity and vertical movement only.
- Admin surfaces remain calm but prioritise clarity and operational density over decoration.
